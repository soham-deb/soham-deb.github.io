The Turtle
The Loggerhead Sea Turtle gets its name for their large heads and strong jaw muscles, alloeing them to break hard-shelled prey like urchins and clams. Recent studies show that the population of these animals are decreasing slowly, even though they are unlikely to be caught by fishermen.
