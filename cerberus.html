The Cerberus
The Cerberus is a Greek mythological character depicted as a dog with three heads. He is known as the pet of Hades, god of the Underworld. The Cerberus guards the gateway to the Underworld, preventing the dead from leaving. Some legends say that the Cerberus had more than 3 heads and had serpents for its tail.
